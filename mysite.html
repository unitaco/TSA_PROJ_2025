About Us

Founded in 2012 by Cliff and Sarah Smith, The Grove locally sources its ingredients from farms in surrounding areas. The Grove is hyper-fouced on the farm-to-table process and has been since day one. The Grove is home to creative dishes with influences from cultures all over the world. We continue to keep our founding ideals of making good food for good people.
